**Tekken: Blood Vengeance** is a 2011 CGI animated film produced by Namco Bandai and Digital Frontier. Released alongside Tekken Hybrid for PlayStation 3, it explores the conflict between Jin Kazama and Lars Alexandersson over the secret of the M-Cell.

## Details

- Runtime: 96 minutes
- Studio: Digital Frontier
- Languages: Japanese, English
- Release: Theatrical (Japan) + Blu-ray worldwide
