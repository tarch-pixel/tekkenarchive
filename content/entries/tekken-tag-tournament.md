**Tekken Tag Tournament** launched as an arcade title and later as a PlayStation 2 launch title. While non-canonical, it is beloved for its expansive roster pulling from all prior games and the strategic depth of tag-team mechanics.

## Highlights

- 39 playable characters
- Tag Assault system for combo extensions
- Bowling mini-game on PS2 version
- PS2 launch title in Japan and North America
