**Tekken: The Motion Picture** is a 1998 Japanese animated OVA that serves as a loose adaptation of the first and second games. It focuses on the conflict between Kazuya Mishima and his father Heihachi, with Jun Kazama as a central protagonist.

## Details

- Runtime: ~60 minutes
- Studio: Group TAC
- Director: Kunihisa Sugishima
- Languages: Japanese (original), English dub available

## Story

Jun Kazama enters the King of Iron Fist Tournament to stop a bioweapon conspiracy orchestrated by the Mishima Zaibatsu. The film explores Kazuya's tragic origin and his devil transformation.
