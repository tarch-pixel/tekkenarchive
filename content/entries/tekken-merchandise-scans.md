An extensive archive of **Tekken merchandise and promotional collectibles**, preserving rare and out-of-print items for fans and historians.

## Archive Contents

- Arcade flyers and promotional posters (T1–T8)
- Limited edition packaging scans
- Tournament program booklets
- EVO and major tournament merchandise
- Namco promotional catalogs
- Action figures and statue reference photos

## Preservation Note

Many of these items are no longer in production. This archive aims to preserve Tekken's physical cultural history for future generations.
