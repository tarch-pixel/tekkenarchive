**Tekken 2** launched in arcades in August 1995 and saw a massive expansion in roster and storytelling depth. The game introduced sub-bosses and more elaborate character endings, cementing the series as a narrative-driven franchise.



## Improvements Over T1

- Improved graphics and animations
- Added throw escapes and reversals
- More detailed character stories
