**Tekken 3** is frequently cited as one of the greatest video games of all time. Released in 1997, it introduced the sidestep system which added true depth to the 3D fighting gameplay, along with a mostly new cast set 19 years after Tekken 2.
## Legacy

Sold over 8 million copies on PlayStation alone and inspired a generation of fighting game players worldwide.
