The original **Tekken** was released in arcades in December 1994, developed and published by Namco. It was one of the first fully 3D fighting games, revolutionizing the genre with its unique four-button control scheme and independent limb system.

## Characters

- Kazuya Mishima
- Paul Phoenix
- Marshall Law
- Nina Williams
- Jack
- Michelle Chang
- Yoshimitsu
- King

## Technical Details

- Resolution: 512×480
- Polygon-based 3D engine
- 60 FPS gameplay
