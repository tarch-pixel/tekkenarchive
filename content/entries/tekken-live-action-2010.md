The **2010 Tekken live-action film** was directed by Dwight H. Little and stars Jon Foo as Jin Kazama. Set in a dystopian future, the film follows Jin's journey from the Anvil slums into the brutal Iron Fist Tournament controlled by the Tekken Corporation.

## Details

- Runtime: 92 minutes
- Director: Dwight H. Little
- Stars: Jon Foo, Kelly Overton, Cary-Hiroyuki Tagawa

## Cast

- Jon Foo as Jin Kazama
- Luke Goss as Steve Fox
- Ian Anthony Dale as Kazuya Mishima
- Cary-Hiroyuki Tagawa as Heihachi Mishima
